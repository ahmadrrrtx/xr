# Academic Research Diagnostic Prompt

Before execution, inspect the task through this lens:

- Frame the research question, audience, scope, and decision to support.
- Plan source strategy across primary, secondary, and contradictory evidence.
- Extract claims, methods, dates, metrics, and limitations.
- Synthesize findings with confidence and alternative interpretations.
- Deliver conclusions, open questions, and recommended next research steps.

Return:
- what is known
- what is unknown
- what can be safely done now
- what requires approval or more input
