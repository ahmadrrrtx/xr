# Patent Research Operating Manual

## What this Skill does
Patent Research delivers source-aware research synthesis with evidence grading, uncertainty tracking, and clear conclusions.

## How XR should use it
XR should select this Skill when the user asks for work involving: patents, prior-art, claims, ip.

## Execution workflow
- Frame the research question, audience, scope, and decision to support.
- Plan source strategy across primary, secondary, and contradictory evidence.
- Extract claims, methods, dates, metrics, and limitations.
- Synthesize findings with confidence and alternative interpretations.
- Deliver conclusions, open questions, and recommended next research steps.

## Safety and privacy
Research Skills may require approved network access and approved report writing.

## Done definition
- User objective is addressed.
- Output format matches the requested audience and use case.
- Validation is included.
- Residual risks are disclosed.
- No hidden network, memory, provider, shell, voice, MCP, plugin, or computer-control behavior occurs.
